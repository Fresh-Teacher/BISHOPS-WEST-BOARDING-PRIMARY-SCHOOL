About Us

This page has a brief description of Bishop's West Boarding Primary School


Bishop's West is a Day and Boarding Primary School located at Mukono Hill Village near St. Phillips and Andrews Cathedral Church.

Our school is a Christian founded school with a strong religious background which impacts on the spiritual growth of out learners.
